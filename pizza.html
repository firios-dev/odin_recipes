Pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Oregano
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Mix the water flour and salt.
  2. Knead that dough like a champ.
  3. Let the dough sit. Standing will strain its little muscles.
  4. After an hour of sitting, stretch that dough into a flat circle.
  5. Smash tomatoes into a paste.
  6. Add oregano to taste.
  7. Spread sauce onto flattened dough.
  8. Sprinkle shredded cheese onto the sauce.
  9. Add enough pepperoni so the pizza tastes good.
  10. Bake at 533 Kelvin until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted, usually 12-15 minutes.
